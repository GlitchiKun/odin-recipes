Chocolate cake

Description

A sweet and marvelous desert for young and old alike.

Ingredients

  • 200g of chocolate
  • 100g of butter
  • 100g of sugar
  • 50g of flour
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C. In a saucepan, melt the chocolate and butter cut into pieces over a very low heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add the sugar, eggs and flour. Mix together.
  3. Add the chocolate/butter mixture. Mix well.
  4. Grease the tin with butter using a sheet of kitchen paper and dust with flour, then pour in the cake mixture.
  5. Bake in the oven for about 20 minutes.
  6. When you take it out of the oven, the cake may not look quite cooked. This is normal. Leave it to cool, then turn it out of the tin.Cover and bake at 375° for 30 minutes.
