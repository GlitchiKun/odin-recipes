Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is made of layers of pasta with meats, tomato sauce, béchamel sauce, vegetables and cheeses between them.

This dish can be served alone, or with a side dish such as a salad.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Meat Sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish.
  2. Add a layer of a boiled lasagna sheet.
  3. Spread 1/3 of the cheese on top and add some béchamel sauce.
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  5. repeat the same layers two more times.
  6. Top with cheese.
  7. Cover and bake at 375° for 30 minutes.
